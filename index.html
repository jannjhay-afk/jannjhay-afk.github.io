Abstract

Today's digital infrastructure is broken: it can't reliably distinguish humans from bots, lacks built-in safeguards for AI interactions, and forces a binary choice between invasive surveillance and unchecked chaos. Islah Nexus proposes a three-layer mobile OS architecture that enforces truth through cryptography while protecting privacy, embeds constitutional AI principles at the core, and establishes verified human identity without centralization.

The system comprises: (1) The Void—a tamper-proof logging engine; (2) The Architect—an offline AI for user modeling; (3) The Surface—integration with online services. This makes deception infeasible, guides vulnerable users without monitoring, and paves the way for "The Great Cleaning."
